About Hunter Krieger

I'm a web developer, game designer, musician, bibliophile and blogger in his late 20's, trying to create and build things both economically valuable and/or intrinsically satisfying just to pass the time constructively before my mind and body gives out.

I've picked up web programming over the past 5 or so years using web technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python etc... and so far I've built a number of websites for others and myself (Including this one. Also see brineyconstructionservices.com and www.winamacpowershow.com)

I'm an obsessive reader who reads psychology, history, economics, video games, music etc. and my goal is to read a book a week and write about it. I will fall short many times but it's a good goal to shoot for.

I'm a musician who has played guitar and piano for about a decade. I'm not a musical prodigy but I know my scales, chords, arpeggios, progressions etc. and can play by ear.

I've had a pet interest in the medium of video games and have dabbled in game development as well. Even though I've learned a lot having made a few unfinished prototypes and one published level of a platformer in Unity and C#, I've regretably had to put that hobby on hiatus to focus on other things. But in no time I look forward to picking it up again using C# and Monogame game framework. Although people tend to dismiss video games as a time-wasting hobby, I've found it to be an interesting artform to study up on.

Thank you for tuning into hckrieger.com. If there's anything you need to tell me then contact me by email at hunterk31@hotmail.com and I'll look forward to hearing from you!
